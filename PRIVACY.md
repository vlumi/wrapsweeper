# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-24_

**Donpa Squad does not collect any personal data.**

The app runs entirely on your device:

- **No data leaves your device.** Donpa Squad makes no network connections —
  there is no account, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no
  third-party SDKs.
- **What's stored locally.** Your settings (appearance, handedness, language,
  last board), per-board scores (best times and games cleared), and any
  in-progress game are saved on your device (via the system's standard local
  storage) so they persist between launches. This information stays on your
  device, is never transmitted, and is removed if you delete the app.
- **No tracking.** The app does not track you across apps or websites and does
  not use any device identifiers for advertising.
- **Children.** Because the app collects no data at all, it collects none from
  children either.
